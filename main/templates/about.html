Mission & Vision

Mission

Our mission at Iskonect is to empower the academic community of the Polytechnic University of the Philippines - Quezon City campus through cutting-edge and user-friendly digital solutions. We are dedicated to providing a singular, accessible platform that enhances connectivity, streamlines processes, and elevates the overall educational experience for both faculty and students. Iskonect aims to be the catalyst for positive change, fostering innovation and collaboration within the university community.

Vision

At Iskonect, we envision a future where the Polytechnic University of the Philippines - Quezon City campus stands at the forefront of digital education. Our vision is to see Iskonect as the cornerstone of a seamless and transformative academic experience, transcending traditional boundaries. We aspire to be the catalyst that propels the university community into a realm of technological excellence, fostering a culture of continual innovation, collaboration, and adaptability. Iskonect aims to shape a future where education is not just accessible but enriched, empowering individuals to thrive in a rapidly evolving global landscape. We envision Iskonect as more than a platform; it's a driving force propelling the academic community towards unparalleled heights of success and excellence.
