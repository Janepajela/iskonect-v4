A platform dedicated exclusively to the diverse range of services offered by PUPQC.

ISKONECT is a collaborative effort between PUPQC administrators, faculty, and BSIT 4th year Students. Our team is dedicated to enhancing your experience within PUPQC by leveraging technology to streamline tasks, facilitate communication, and provide you with the tools you need to succeed academically and professionally. At our core, we are driven by the pursuit of efficiency. Our main objective is simple yet profound:

  • Streamline transactions for maximum efficiency.
  • Put everything you need at your fingertips for easy accessibility.
  • Ensure everything is well-organized for a seamless experience.

In our journey, every action is a testament to our commitment. We believe in making a meaningful impact, and our dedication echoes through every initiative, ensuring an educational experience that goes beyond the ordinary.
